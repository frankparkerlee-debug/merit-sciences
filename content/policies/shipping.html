Shipping Policy

 

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

 

Domestic Shipping (United States)

Standard shipping: 3-5 business days. Free on orders over $250.

Expedited shipping: 1-2 business days. Available at checkout for an additional fee.

 

All domestic orders are shipped via USPS or UPS with tracking provided.

 

Temperature-Sensitive Products

Compound products are shipped with appropriate cold-chain packaging when required. During warmer months, insulated packaging and ice packs are included at no additional charge.

 

International Shipping

We currently ship within the United States only. International shipping is not available at this time.

 

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. Please allow 24 hours for tracking to update.

 

Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us at support@meritpeptides.com within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and reship or refund your order.
