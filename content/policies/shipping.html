Compound Stability in Transit

Compounds ship as lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder in sealed glass vials. The lyophilized format is stable at ambient temperatures and does not require cold-chain shipping — typical sealed shelf-life is ≥24 months when stored at -20°C, with significant stability at standard ambient ranges throughout transit. Bacteriostatic water ships at ambient and remains stable. Once a compound is reconstituted with bacteriostatic water, the resulting solution should be refrigerated at 2–8°C and used within 30 days.
